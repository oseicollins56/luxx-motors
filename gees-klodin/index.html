Our Story

More Than a Stitch — It's a Craft

Gee's Klodin was born from a passion for fabric, fit, and fashion. Located in the heart of Dome, Accra, our studio has been transforming ideas into beautifully crafted garments for over a decade.

From traditional Kente ensembles to elegant Western-style pieces, every garment that leaves our studio carries our signature touch — precise cuts, careful finishing, and a fit that makes you feel your best.

  • Expert tailors with over a decade of hands-on experience
  • Specialising in both traditional African and modern Western wear
  • A welcoming learning environment for beginners and apprentices
  • Every garment is custom-measured and hand-crafted to your specifications
  • Proud to serve Dome and the wider Accra community

Apprenticeship Programme

Our full apprenticeship is designed to turn passionate beginners into skilled, market-ready tailors and fashion designers. Here's how the journey works:

1

Introduction & Basics (Month 1–2)

Fabric types, tools, hand sewing, basic machine operation, and your first simple garment.

2

Pattern Making & Cutting (Month 3–4)

Learn to take measurements, draft patterns, and cut fabric with precision for different garment types.

3

Construction & Finishing (Month 5–8)

Construct complete garments — dresses, trousers, suits, traditional wear — with professional finishing techniques.

4

Graduation & Certification

Complete a showcase project, receive your certificate, and launch your career with the skills to start your own business.

🎓 Limited Spots Available

We accept a small number of apprentices each intake to ensure quality training. Contact us to check availability and secure your place.
