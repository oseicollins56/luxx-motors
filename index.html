The Process

BID. BUY. SHIP.

Three simple steps. Zero headaches. Your car delivered anywhere in West Africa and beyond.

01
🔍

Request & Search

Tell us the make, model, year, and budget. We search US auctions and dealer networks to find your exact vehicle at the best price.

02
🏷️

Bid & Secure

We bid competitively on your behalf at top US auctions — Copart, IAAI, Manheim — and secure your vehicle at the right price.

03
🚢

Ship & Track

We handle all export documentation, port logistics, and shipping. You track your vehicle every step of the way to its destination port.

04
🔑

Receive & Drive

Your car arrives at your destination port, cleared and ready. We support you through local customs clearance documentation too.
